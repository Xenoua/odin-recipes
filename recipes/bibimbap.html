Bibimbap Recipe

Bibimbap

Bibimbap is a Korean dish which directly translates to "mixed rice with meat and assorted vegetables". Bibimbap is one of my favorite korean dishes due to the variation of flavors it serves depending on the ingredients used.

Ingredients:

Meat and Meat Sauce

  • Beef mince
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Brown sugar
  • Minced garlic

Vegetables and others

  • Seasoned spinach
  • Seasoned bean sprouts
  • Shiitake mushroom
  • Carrots
  • Fine sea salt
  • Steamed rice
  • Eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Toasted seasoned seaweed

Bibimbap Sauce

  • Gochujang
  • Sesame oil
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Roasted Sesame seeds
  • Apple vinegar
  • Minced garlic

Steps on how to make Bibimbap:

  1. Prepare and cook ingredients below:

    2. -For meat, mix the beef mince with the meat sauce listed above. Marinate the meat for about 30 mins while you are working on other ingredients to enhance the flavour. Add some cooking oil into a wok and cook the meat on medium high to high heat. It takes about 3 to 5 mins to thoroughly cook it.

    -Mix the bibimbap sauce ingredients in a bowl.

    -Cook spinach and bean sprouts.

    -Rinse, peel and julienne the carrots. Add some cooking oil and 1/4 tsp of fine sea salt in a wok and cook the carrots on medium high to high heat for 2 to 3 mins.

    -Clean/rinse the shiitake mushrooms and thinly slice them. Add some cooking oil and 1/4 tsp of fine sea salt in a wok and cook the mushrooms on medium high to high heat until they are all cooked. (It takes 2 to 3 mins.)

    -Make fried eggs. (While sunny side up is common, you can make them per your preference.)

  2. Put the rice into a bowl and add the meat, assorted vegetables, seasoned seaweed, bibimbap sauce, and the egg on top of the rice. Serve.
  3. To eat, mix the ingredients in the bowl, and enjoy!
